LPC-D240 Series

LPC-D240 Series is a mission-critical 24" P-cap defense Panel PC powered by Intel® 14th Gen processors and secured with NIST-approved Post-Quantum Cryptography (FIPS 203/204/205). Certified to MIL-STD-810H, 461G, 1275E, and 704F, it offers a 1500-nit anti-glare, optically bonded sunlight-readable display, rugged D38999 connectors, IP65 front protection, and -40℃ to 70℃ "Cold Start" capability. Designed for shipboard CIC, bridge consoles, shore-based C2 and C4ISR centers, aircraft maintenance hangars, and USV/UGV platforms, it ensures robust reliability in naval, airborne, and ground command operations.

Product Highlights - Advantages and Benefits
  • PQC-Proof Quantum Security (Optional by Customer Request): Integrates NIST FIPS 203/204/205 compliant Post-Quantum Cryptography (PQC) to protect sensitive data against "Harvest Now, Decrypt Later" threats.
  • Military-Grade Certification: Compliant with MIL-STD-704F, MIL-STD-1275E, and MIL-STD-461G for EMC/EMI compliance, as well as MIL-STD-810H for shock and vibration resilience.
  • Extreme Environment Reliability: Engineered for -40℃ to 70℃ environments with "Cold Start" technology, enabling immediate system boot-up in freezing conditions.
  • Superior Outdoor Readability: Equipped with a 1500-nit ultra-bright display, optical bonding, and anti-glare technology for crystal-clear visibility under direct sunlight.
  • Enhanced Durability and Resistance: The full metal chassis and tempered cover glass provides superior mechanical strength, offering exceptional protection against physical damage, high-impact forces, and heavy vibrations common in industrial settings.
  • Secure Connectivity: D38999/D26482 military connectors for all I/Os. Prevent accidental cable disconnection.
  • Stable Supply: 10-year longevity support and proactive notification of EOL.
